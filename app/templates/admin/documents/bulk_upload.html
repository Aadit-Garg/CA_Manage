How to Bulk Upload

To automatically map files to the correct clients, you must include the client's Upload ID anywhere in the PDF filename.

  • The Upload ID is a unique identifier (e.g., CLI-4X9M2Q) found on the Client Profile page.
  • Example filenames: CLI-4X9M2Q_Income_Tax_Return_2023.pdf or Invoice_CLI-4X9M2Q.pdf
  • The system will automatically detect the financial year (e.g. 2023-24) and document type if present in the filename.

Upload Documents

Drag & drop PDFs here or click to browse. The system will automatically map them to clients based on the Upload UUID in the filename.
